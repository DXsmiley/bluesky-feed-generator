Who Is Included On The Feed?

Currently, "furries" constitutes anybody who follows one of a short list of major furry accounts. It does not dynamically update, and ends up including some people who are not furries. This is a temporary solution before something better is built.

How is the feed ranked?

Each post is scored individually, depending on its age, and the amount of positive-sentiment interaction its getting

y=L+b(T+t)g

Where L is the number of likes and T is the age of the post.

b is presently 5, t is "two hours" and g is 1.85

Remember that posts older than 48 hours will not show up on the feed
