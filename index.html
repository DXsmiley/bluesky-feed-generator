What Is Included On The Feed?

Only posts from the last 96 hours (~4 days) are shown.

Currently, "furries" constitutes anybody who follows one of a short list of major furry accounts. It does not dynamically update, and ends up including some people who are not furries. Additionally, small number of mutelists is used to exclude accounts from the feeds. This is a temporary solution before something better is built.
